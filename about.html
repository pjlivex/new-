About Retro Games Hub

Welcome to Retro Games Hub, your go-to destination for classic HTML5 games! We offer a collection of timeless games that you can play directly in your browser, no downloads required.

Our mission is to bring back the nostalgia of retro gaming while providing a modern, accessible experience. All games are built with HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript for smooth gameplay on any device.

Features include leaderboards, favorites, and a responsive design that works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
