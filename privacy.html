Privacy Policy

Retro Games Hub respects your privacy. We collect minimal data: player names and scores are stored locally in your browser. No personal information is collected or shared.

We use localStorage for game progress, favorites, and leaderboards. You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings.

For the optional Firebase integration, scores may be stored on Firebase servers. Please refer to Firebase's privacy policy.
